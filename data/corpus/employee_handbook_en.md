# Employee Handbook

## Annual Leave
Full-time employees receive 18 paid annual leave days per calendar year after probation. Leave requests should be submitted at least five working days before the planned start date. Emergency leave can be requested afterwards with manager approval.

## Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to two days per week when the role allows it. The employee must keep calendar availability accurate and follow information security rules. Remote work from outside the employment country requires HR and Legal approval before travel.

## Expense Claims
Business travel expenses must be submitted within 30 calendar days after the trip ends. Receipts are required for lodging, transport, and meals above the local threshold. Claims submitted after 30 days require Finance exception approval.

## Conduct
Employees must not disclose confidential internal information to external parties. Requests from media, regulators, or external auditors must be redirected to Legal or Compliance.

## 远程办公（中文摘要）
在岗位允许的情况下，员工每周最多可以远程办公两天。员工需要保持日历状态准确，并遵守信息安全要求。如果从雇佣国家以外的地点远程办公，必须提前获得 HR 和 Legal 批准。
